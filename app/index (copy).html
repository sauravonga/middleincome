TRAPS & TRANSITIONS

Some economies are trapped into perpetual cycle of stagnant growth while some are able to transition to high income

TRAPS

An economy squeezed between the low-wage poor-country competitors that dominate in mature industries and the rich-country innovators that dominate in industries undergoing rapid technological change.

TRANSITIONS

Successfully moving an economy from middle income to rich, under proper circumstances and policies by balancing manufacturing competitiveness and fostering a culture of technological innovation.

INDICATORS OF TRANSITIONS

A multi-pronged approach to policy making ensures that an economy is able to transition to high income.
